What both share (the "Structured Brutalist" DNA):

  • Bold, oversized typography with tight letter-spacing — immediate visual impact
  • Monospace accents for metadata — technical without being "developer theme"
  • Table/list project layout instead of cards — unconventional, scannable, confident
  • Intentional asymmetry — grid-based but not symmetrical
  • Minimal decoration — no gradients, no glows, no rounded cards. Just type + space + rules
